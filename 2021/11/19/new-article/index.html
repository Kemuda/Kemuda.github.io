Amber X Canal

Dear Bennett and Justine,

With experience in several early stage start-ups, I’m skilled at wearing multiple hats, dealing with ambiguity and, where needed, figuring it out on my own. I’m excited to bring my contacts, operational insights, and commercial acumen to help Canal take bold strides forward.

One of my obsessions is working out how brands can grow. Understanding customers is naturally central to that goal. In my most recent experience at Haul, which aims to build an ecommerce marketplace similar to Taobao in the US, I was the one trawling Reddit, Depop, and relevant Discord channels trying to decipher their behavioural patterns, when we were initially targeting Gen-Z girls and fashion merchants. Those insights were directly employed by the leadership to help make key decisions in areas like merchant vetting, ultimately boosting our sales 5X in 2 months.

To deepen my understanding of online retail, this year I independently opened and maintained both a Depop store and a Shopify site. I tasted every responsibility in the retail industry and gained all kinds of operational insights, including communicating with the full range of stakeholders in the e-commerce sphere.

As a rudimentary analyst in venture capital, one of my core responsibilities was deal-sourcing. Through an ongoing process of routinely cold-contacting or asking my contacts to make introductions, I’ve honed an ability to pinpoint people in key positions, reach out through multiple channels, and build meaningful connections. This skillset will transfer directly into effective brand acquisition for Canal.

Harnessing my experience leading social media campaigns and a sharp analytical mind, I’ve developed an effective approach to maximizing social reach. At Haul, through a systematic influencer program that I initiated and structured, in only two months we grew our collective social reach from zero to 15,600. I can increase Canal’s reach and help attract hundreds of new clients.

Lastly, I’d like to say how exciting it is to find a company with a philosophy that feels so aligned with my own. I have worked with hundreds of brand owners, always appreciated the human connections, and taken great pleasure in helping them take their ventures forward. I love to serve people and would be delighted to help Canal and its customers fulfil their ambitions.

I have composed a presentation around how I would approach growing the Canal network and would be delighted to take you through my thoughts in an interview.

Best,

Amber
